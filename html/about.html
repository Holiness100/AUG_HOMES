    About AUG HOMES

    Welcome to AUG HOMES, which stands for Agada Unekwuojo Godswill HOMES.
    We are a leading real estate company specialising in land sales, house purchases and rentals, and apartment rentals.

    History

    AUG HOMES was established in 2024 by Mr. Godswill Agada, who currently serves as the CEO.
    The company was born out of Mr. Agada's personal experience and concern for the challenges people face in securing homes, land, and rental properties.
    During his time as a student, Mr. Agada encountered difficulties finding an apartment, despite paying substantial agency fees.
    This experience inspired him to create AUG HOMES, with the mission of simplifying the real estate process and providing accessible and reliable services to those in need.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to bridge the gap between agents and clients, ensuring a transparent and trustworthy real estate experience.
    We aim to significantly reduce instances of fraud, such as purchasing government-owned land unknowingly or buying properties without proper ownership verification.
    Additionally, we are committed to minimizing the stress and complexities associated with finding apartments, houses, or land.

    Our Vision

    Our vision is to become one of the leading real estate companies in the country and eventually worldwide.
    We aim to make property acquisition effortless and accessible for everyone.
